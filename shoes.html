Air dior (Jordan x Dior Collaboration)

AUTHENTICITY Years ago, before the Air Dior Jordan 1s hit every sneakerhead’s feed, there were rumors. In 2017, Kris Van Assche, then Dior Men’s creative director, posted an image of the Dior logo in the Nike font sitting atop a Swoosh. The caption? “JUST DO DIOR.” Van Assche quickly deleted the photo, but it was too late: whispers on the internet about a potential collaboration abounded, and continued.
